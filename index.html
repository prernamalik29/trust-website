About Us

About Our Trust

Olympian Anuj International Trust is dedicated to transforming lives through sports, healthcare, and nutrition. Founded with a vision to create a healthier and more active society, we work tirelessly to bring positive change to communities across India.

Our mission is to empower individuals, especially children and youth, by providing them with opportunities to excel in sports, access quality healthcare, and receive nutritious food.

  • Promoting sports excellence and physical fitness
  • Providing free healthcare to underserved areas
  • Fighting child malnutrition through meal programs
