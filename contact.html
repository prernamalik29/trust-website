FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to the trust?

You can donate through our website's donation page, via bank transfer, or by visiting our office. We accept online payments, UPI, and cheques. All donations are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G.

How can I become a volunteer?

We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds! Simply fill out the contact form with "Volunteer Opportunity" as the subject, or call us directly. We'll arrange an orientation session to help you get started.

Can I organize an event in partnership with the trust?

Absolutely! We're always looking for partners to expand our reach. Contact us with your proposal, and our team will evaluate the potential collaboration based on alignment with our mission.

How is my donation used?

We maintain complete transparency in our operations. Your donations are used for our three core programs: Sports Events, Healthcare, and Food for Children. We publish annual reports detailing our expenditures and impact.
