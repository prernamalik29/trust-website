Who We Are

About Olympian Anuj International Trust

Olympian Anuj International Trust was founded with a vision to create a healthier, more active, and well-nourished society. Named after an Olympian athlete who understands the transformative power of sports, we are dedicated to empowering communities across India.

Our trust operates on the belief that every individual, regardless of their socioeconomic background, deserves access to sports opportunities, quality healthcare, and nutritious food. Through our three core programs, we work tirelessly to bring positive change to thousands of lives every year.

  • Promoting sports excellence and physical fitness among youth
  • Providing free healthcare services to underserved communities
  • Fighting child malnutrition through daily meal programs
  • Training aspiring athletes from underprivileged backgrounds
Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through comprehensive sports programs, accessible healthcare services, and nutritious food initiatives. We strive to create opportunities for growth, health, and well-being for those who need it most.

Our Vision

A world where every child has the opportunity to pursue their athletic dreams, access quality healthcare, and never go to bed hungry. We envision healthy, active communities where potential is not limited by circumstance.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, excellence, and inclusivity guide everything we do. We believe in transparent operations, community involvement, and creating sustainable impact through our programs.

Why Choose Us

What Makes Us Different

With over a decade of experience in grassroots-level work, we have built strong relationships with communities across India. Our approach is unique because we focus on sustainable, long-term impact rather than short-term aid.

Community-Driven

We work closely with local communities to understand their needs and design programs that truly make a difference.

Transparent Operations

Every rupee donated is accounted for. We maintain complete transparency in our financial operations.

Proven Track Record

Over 10 years of successful programs with measurable impact on thousands of lives.
