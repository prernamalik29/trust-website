कुश्ती प्रतियोगिता 2026 Wrestling Competition

The 24th edition of Kushti Utthan Samiti's wrestling championship organised by Olympian Anuj International Trust. Over 500 young athletes competed across multiple weight categories in boys' and girls' divisions.

Date 8 March 2026 (Sunday)
Time 10:00 AM onwards
Venue Modern Public Inter College, NH-58, Roorkee Road, Muzaffarnagar
Participants 500+ Athletes

Event Highlights

  • Chief Guest: Dr. DS Chauhan I.P.S., Retd. DGP UP, Director UPCA
  • Boys categories: Under 12, Under 15, Under 17 (35–92 kg)
  • Girls categories (Open): 50 kg & 55 kg
  • Padmashri Guru Hanuman Ji — Patron
